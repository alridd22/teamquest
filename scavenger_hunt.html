💡 Photo Tips for Maximum Doubloons

  • ⚠️ FRAME REQUIRED FOR SCORING - The treasure MUST be clearly visible inside the printed magical frame from your booklet. Photos without the frame will score 0 doubloons!
  • Ensure good lighting and focus - Natural daylight works best
  • Make the treasure the main subject - Fill the frame with your find
  • Position clearly within the magical frame boundaries - The entire treasure must be inside the frame borders
  • For date-specific items (like the 2023 coin) - Make sure the date is clearly readable within the frame
  • Avoid multiple different items in the same photo - Focus on one treasure at a time
  • Hold steady - Avoid blurry photos by keeping your device still
