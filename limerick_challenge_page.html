📝 Verse Vault Scoring
How Terry's poetic masters evaluate your limericks
🎵 Rhyme Scheme & Rhythm
Up to 20 Points
Perfect limericks follow the classic AABBA rhyme pattern with bouncy, musical rhythm that flows like a sea shanty.
We look for: Lines 1, 2, 5 rhyming together • Lines 3, 4 forming a shorter rhyming pair • Anapestic meter (da-da-DUM rhythm)
✨ Creativity & Originality
Up to 20 Points
The most treasure goes to imaginative tales with unexpected twists, clever wordplay, and original storytelling that surprises and delights.
We treasure: Unique topics • Surprise endings • Clever wordplay and puns • Vivid imagery • Creative use of language
😄 Humor & Entertainment
Up to 20 Points
Limericks that make us chuckle, smile, or laugh out loud earn the highest entertainment scores. Wit and playfulness are worth their weight in doubloons!
We enjoy: Funny situations • Clever puns • Absurd or silly scenarios • Playful language • Witty observations

🏆 Treasure Chest Scoring Bands

50-60 Doubloons
Legendary Verse: Flawless rhythm, brilliantly clever or hilarious, highly original masterpiece
35-49 Doubloons
Worthy Poem: Good rhythm and structure, shows creativity or humor, well-crafted
25-34 Doubloons
Decent Attempt: Follows limerick rules but lacks significant impact or originality
15-24 Doubloons
Basic Effort: Some limerick elements present but rhythm issues or limited creativity
1-14 Doubloons
Needs Work: Broken structure, significant rhythm problems, or very low effort

💡 Tips for Maximum Doubloons

  • Perfect your rhythm - Read aloud to ensure bouncy, musical flow
  • Nail the rhyme scheme - AABBA pattern with strong rhymes
  • Add unexpected twists - Surprise endings earn extra treasure
  • Be playfully absurd - Limericks love silly, exaggerated scenarios
  • Use clever wordplay - Puns and double meanings delight our judges
  • Keep it fun - Entertainment value is worth serious doubloons!
