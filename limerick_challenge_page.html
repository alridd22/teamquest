Terry's Verse Vault

Craft clever limericks and earn doubloons for your wit!

Ah, a poet approaches! Welcome to me verse vault, where words be worth their weight in doubloons! Craft me a limerick that tickles the funny bone and shows yer creative spirit. The wittier and more clever, the greater yer treasure reward shall be!

📜 Limerick Treasure Rules

  • 5 lines total: Lines 1, 2, 5 must rhyme together (A-A-A pattern)
  • Lines 3, 4 rhyme: Shorter lines that rhyme with each other (B-B pattern)
  • Rhythm matters: Bouncy, musical flow like a sea shanty!
  • Be creative: Funny, clever, or surprising endings earn the most treasure!
  • Earn up to 60 doubloons based on creativity, humour, and poetic technique
