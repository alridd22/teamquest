💡 Tips for Maximum Heart Treasures

  • Think creatively - Personalize your kindness to the recipient's needs
  • Document the impact - Show how your kindness affected others
  • Put in real effort - Time and preparation show genuine care
  • Focus on authenticity - Genuine acts score higher than staged ones
  • Tell the full story - Explain the situation, action, and outcome
  • Make it meaningful - Address real needs or brighten someone's day significantly
